Full
Double

Ingredients

2 lbs. mini lasagna or bow tie pasta
0.5 cups. Butter
2 lbs. Pizza meat or italian sausage
2 cups Cream
104 oz. Spaghetti Sauce
2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1 Tbsp. Italian Seasoning
2 lbs. Caesar Blend Cheese (Parmesan, Romano, Asiago)
2 lbs. Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • First(pre-prep)
  • Second(prep a)
  • Third(prep b)
  • Fourth(Bake)
  • Fifth(Eat!)
Preheat oven to 375
Start to boil water for pasta

Pasta:

When your water is boiling add noodles. Subract 2-3 min. from recommeded cook time so they are slightly under done. Drain and set aside. (optional, stir in a little olive oil to keep noodles from sticking)

White Sauce:

Over medium-high heat, add butter to the sauce pan. Once the butter is melted add the cream, garlic, and italian seasonings constantly stir and bring to a light boil. When it starts to boil slowly stir in caesar blend cheese, when all of the cheese is added and melted, remove from heat.

Sausage:

Over medium-high heat, brown italian sausage and drain fat. It is ok if the meat is slightly underdone because it will be baked.

Pasta:

Stir white sauce into pasta.

Layer:

In greased cake pan, add a thin layer red sauce, then continue to layer ingredients in this order.
  • Noodles
  • Red sauce
  • Sausage
  • Mozzarella

Continue to layer until pan is nearly full. If enough ingredients remain, repeat this process in an additional cake pan.
Bake at 375 for 45 minutes or until bubbling and lightly brown.
Enjoy!

Supplies

You will need:
  • Cake pan(s).
  • A large frying pan
  • A large Sauce pan
  • A large pot
